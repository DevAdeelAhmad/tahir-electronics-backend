## Core E-commerce Collections
1. **Product** 
   - Stores all product information, pricing, and images
   - Will link to categories and variants

2. **ProductCategory**
   - Classification of products (e.g., mushroom grow bags, cocoa fertilizer)
   - Hierarchical structure for main/sub-categories

3. **ProductVariant**
   - For products with different options/sizes/prices

4. **Order**
   - Contains order information, status, and customer details
   - Links to order items

5. **OrderItem**
   - Individual items within an order
   - References products and their quantities

6. **Customer**
   - User profile data for customers
   - Shipping/billing information

## Content Collections
7. **Page**
   - For static pages (Home, About, Services, FAQ)
   - Flexible content using Strapi's components

8. **Blog/Article**
   - For news, updates, or educational content
   - Will need categories and possibly tags

## Integration & Support Collections
9. **Vendor**
   - Information about vendors (Garden City Fungi, HortiRIGHT Co)
   - Will link to products

10. **Inventory**
    - Stock levels for products
    - Could include reorder points, availability status

11. **ContactRequest**
    - Stores form submissions from customers
    - Support tickets or general inquiries

## Configuration Collections
12. **SiteSettings**
    - Global site configuration
    - Contact information, social links, etc.

13. **SEO**
    - For storing SEO-related information for pages and products

14. **ShippingMethod**
    - Different shipping options available

15. **PaymentMethod**
    - Payment options and configuration

## Single Types (not collections)
1. **Global**
   - Site-wide settings and configuration

2. **About**
   - Company information
